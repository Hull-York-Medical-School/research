How we’re doing it

We believe ground-breaking discoveries can only happen in a collaborative and inclusive research environment.

Our unique partnership with the University of Hull and the University of York, brings together world leading experts across a range of disciplines, and offers a great environment in which to conduct research which has discovery and innovation at its heart.

And our partnerships with our regional NHS Trusts and community health organisations, offer a wide clinical base within with to study those conditions which most affect our communities. By working with patients we can improve their health, while developing research that can be applied nationally and globally.
